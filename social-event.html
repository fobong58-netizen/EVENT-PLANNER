Frequently Asked Questions

What does a wedding planner do?

A wedding planner handles all aspects of your wedding, from venue selection to vendor coordination, design, budget management, timeline creation, and on-the-day execution, ensuring a stress-free experience.


When should I hire a wedding planner?

It's best to hire a planner 9-12 months before your wedding. However, we can assist with last-minute planning if needed.


Do you offer post-wedding services?

Yes, we assist with post-event tasks like vendor payments and returning rentals to ensure everything is wrapped up smoothly.


Do you provide rentals?

We don't directly provide rentals, but we can recommend trusted rental companies for decor, furniture, and other wedding essentials.


Does Cisco Events handle destination event logistics?

Absolutely. Cisco Events offers complete destination event management, including travel coordination, accommodation arrangements, local vendor sourcing, and on-site logistics. Their global network and experience ensure that every detail is flawlessly executed, no matter where in the world the event takes place.


Can I make changes to my wedding plan after booking?

Yes, we can accommodate changes and adjust plans as needed.
